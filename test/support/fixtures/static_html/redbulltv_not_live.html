Premier Padel

Padel is the fastest-growing racket sport in the world, with teams of two players competing in an enclosed court where the walls are incorporated into play. Premier Padel, the global competition series for elite players, takes place across 25 worldwide tournaments and 18 countries during the 2024 season. Concluding in Barcelona from December 18-22, the Premier Padel Finals will see the top-ranked pairs battle it out for the final men's and women's trophies!
